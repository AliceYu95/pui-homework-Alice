Introduction

Bumble is a dating app with over 50 million users. Bumble now empowers users to connect with confidence whether they are dating, meeting friends, or networking online.

Linkup is an add-on feature to Bumble that provides personalized recommendations on where to meet, easing the transition from online to offline dating and further engaging users in real life. The physical proximity that offline dating brings is one of the most powerful variables that leads to attraction.
